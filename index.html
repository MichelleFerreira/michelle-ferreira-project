Michelle Ferreira is a Canadian Designer, living and working in Toronto, Ontario. She's currently working at SickKids, the Hospital for Sick Children, as a Graphic Designer. Her passions also include photography, interior design and arts and crafts.

Michelle graduated from Seneca College in Ontario, where she studied Graphic Design. She specializes in print with a particular interest in branding and editorial design. In addition to her full-time engagement, she is available for collaborations and commissions.
